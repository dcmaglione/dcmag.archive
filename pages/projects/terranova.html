TERRA NOVA [BOSTONHACKS 2020 WINNER]

Terra Nova is a high-fidelity 3D game designed and built by a team of friends and me for the BostonHacks 2020 Hackathon. BostonHacks was our first hackathon and helped us build more experience when working with a team in a high-stress situation.


The inspiration for the project was, building a better tomorrow through environmental sustainability.


Check Out the Project Here


HOW TO PLAY

The project begins with having players access the respective website and filling out a survey regarding their daily habits and their impact on environmental sustainability. Players are then given the ability to download the game, supporting Mac or Windows, and enter a 3D world composed of simulated houses. Each house is directly attributed to a user and generated based on their survey answers. They can tour various homes and complete tasks inside their home to reduce an "environmental damage score."


The objective of Terra Nova is simple, the player's home must be as environmentally sustainable as possible. Remember, every little bit counts, and every action the player takes will have a global consequence. There will be a series of tasks that can be completed in a players home based on their survey responses, which were recorded before downloading the game. This game is mulitplayer and was designed to encourage collaboration so players wont be alone!


As more players register and join the game, more homes will manifest creating a community. The community will have an overall sustainability score, so a player making their home more sustainable will simulataneously improve the community environment for everyone.


Collaboration is necessary to keep earth sustainable and humankind healthy. Now get to it, a sustainable world waits for no one.


TECH STACK

Terra Nova is a high-fidelity 3D game that communicates in real-time with multiple APIs and a custom-built web platform. The game uses a Unity3D game engine backend, Google Firebase authentication, Google Sheets data collection, and a static GitHub Pages website.


Listed below are the various languages, frameworks, etc. used in creating the project.

  • Unity3D
  • C#
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Javascript
  • Firebase
  • Google Cloud, Docs, Sheets, Shared Spaces
